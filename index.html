FAQs

How often should I clean air ducts?

Every 3 to 5 years for residential, but more frequently if you have pets, allergies, or recent renovations.

What signs indicate my dryer vent is clogged?

Clothes take longer to dry, the dryer gets unusually hot, or there’s a burning smell during operation.
